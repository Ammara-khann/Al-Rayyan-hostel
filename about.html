About Al Rayyan

Our Story

Al Rayyan Girls Hostel was established with a single mission: to provide female students with a safe, comfortable, and nurturing environment where they can focus on their studies and personal growth.

Located in the prestigious Westridge Campus area, our hostel offers modern amenities combined with traditional hospitality. We take pride in our 24/7 security, nutritious meals, and supportive community.
